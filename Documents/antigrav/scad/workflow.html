Browser CAD workflow

OpenSCAD editor for AI CAD, SCAD files, and STL-ready 3D printing

This is the workflow for scAId. It is built for people who want to write OpenSCAD in the browser, generate geometry from text prompts, iterate on parametric parts quickly, and export clean STL files for slicers and printers.

Write or generate SCAD

Start from a blank .scad file, a reusable template, or a plain-English prompt that the AI turns into parametric code you can inspect and edit directly.

Preview geometry in real time

Render immediately in the browser, inspect faces and dimensions, and refine boolean operations, clearances, and proportions before you export.

Export SCAD to STL

Download STL or OBJ files for 3D printing workflows and keep the source SCAD for future edits, versioning, and repeatable engineering changes.
