CSG Booleans

Union, difference, intersection. Combine primitives into complex parts with real constructive solid geometry.

Parametric Variables

Define dimensions as variables. Use expressions and for-loops. Change one value, update the entire model.

STL Export

One-click download to STL. Send to your slicer, CNC, or 3D printer. No conversion tools needed.

Keyboard-First

Ctrl+Enter to render. Ctrl+S to save. Camera presets on F, T, R, I. Built for the way you already work.

Console & Logs

Integrated console shows parse timing, vertex counts, and errors. Debug your geometry without leaving the editor.

Model Templates

Six built-in templates for common patterns: drilled blocks, trophies, columns, pipe joints. One click to load.
